🧠 The 5 Signals the AI Checks

💰

Amount

Is this amount unusual for this account? A €5,000 withdrawal when you normally spend €50 is a red flag.

📍

Location

Where in the world is this happening? Spending in Dublin and Tokyo in the same hour is impossible.

🏪

Merchant Type

Is this a normal type of shop? A sudden purchase from a crypto exchange raises questions.

🕐

Time of Day

3am transactions at overseas ATMs look very different from 9am coffee purchases.

⚡

Velocity

How many transactions in the last 10 minutes? Fraudsters test cards with small purchases first.
